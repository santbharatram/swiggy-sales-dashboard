# 📊 Swiggy Sales Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive, interactive Excel-based Data Analytics Dashboard analyzing Swiggy's sales, orders, and customer ratings performance across various regions, time frames, and food categories in India.

## 🚀 Repository Name Idea
`swiggy-sales-analytics-dashboard` or `swiggy-excel-performance-insights`

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Excel Dashboard designed to analyze and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) for Swiggy's food delivery operations. The dashboard transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders understand sales trends, customer preferences, regional performance, and top-performing restaurant chains.

---

## 🔑 Key Metrics Analyzed (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** ₹5,30,12,506 (53 Million+)
* **Total Orders:** 1,97,430
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹268.51
* **Average Rating:** 4.34 / 5.0
* **Total Rating Count:** 55,91,574

---

## 🗺️ Dashboard Features & Visualizations

### 1. Interactive Slicers (Filters)
* **Months:** Multi-select filtering from January to August.
* **State:** Regional filtering supporting states like Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, etc.
* **Food Type:** Categorical segmentation between **Veg** and **Non-Veg**.
* **Category:** Deep-dive analysis into specific cuisines (e.g., Arabian Grill, Arabic, etc.).

### 2. Analytical Charts & Breakdown
* **Regional Sales Map:** Geographical visualization of sales density across India.
* **Monthly Sales Trend:** Line chart tracking revenue growth and seasonal fluctuations from Jan (₹68.25L) to Aug (₹67.91L).
* **Daily Sales Analysis:** Line chart depicting weekly distribution showing a major peak on Saturday (₹77.82L).
* **Sales by Food Type:** Doughnut chart illustrating market share—**Veg (64%)** vs. **Non-Veg (36%)**.
* **Top 5 Cities (Bar Chart):** Revenue breakdown by top metropolitan hubs led by **Bengaluru** (₹54,56,798), followed by Lucknow, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and New Delhi.
* **Top 10 Restaurants (Treemap):** Revenue distribution among top food chains including KFC, McDonald's, Pizza Hut, Domino's Pizza, and Burger King.
* **Quarterly Performance Matrix:** Structured table aggregating Sales, Average Ratings, and Orders across Q1, Q2, and Q3.

---

## 📈 Business Insights Extracted
* **Core Market:** Bengaluru is the highest-grossing city, making it a critical hub for operations and marketing campaigns.
* **Dietary Preferences:** Vegetarian food dominates the dataset with a substantial **64%** of total sales share.
* **Weekly Peak:** Operational load and sales peak heavily on **Saturdays**, suggesting target windows for running special promotional campaigns.
* **Stability:** Customer satisfaction remains exceptionally stable with an average rating of **4.34** maintained consistently across all quarters (Q1–Q3).

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Advanced Data Modeling, Pivot Tables, and Pivot Charts.
* **Data Visualization:** Custom Geomaps, Treemaps, Doughnut charts, and Line trends.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated Advanced Slicers and Timeline connections for dynamic data filtering.
* **Data Cleaning:** Cleaned and formatted currency, numbers, and ratings fields for professional reporting.

## 📁 How to Use This Repository
1. Clone this repository or download the `.xlsx` file.
2. Open the file in Microsoft Excel (2019 or newer recommended for Treemap and Map chart compatibility).
3. Use the **Slicers on the left panel** to interactively filter the data by Month, State, Food Type, or Cuisine Category.
